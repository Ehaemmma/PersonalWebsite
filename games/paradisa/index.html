Paradisa

The extraordinary name Paradisa (Nissa for short), belongs to a violin prodigy in an ordinary family. Though her talent brings her family honor and fame, seldom the care and praise from strangers and friends will make it through the glaring light of golden trophies, and shines right on Paradisa herself. She has become extra lonely since her mother passed away.

Therefore, in this ordinary morning in spring, she begins an expedition in her own house. She applies her extraordinary imagination on all these common scenes, with her little companion - a cat. No one knows that everything expected to run on routine is not the same again.
